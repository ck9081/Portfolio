About Me

Developer. Designer.
Accessibility Advocate.

I'm Alex Sharma, a frontend developer with a passion for building websites that are not just visually compelling but genuinely accessible and inclusive. I believe the web should work for everyone.

My work centers on writing clean, semantic HTML5, implementing WCAG 2.1 AA guidelines, and creating interfaces that score perfectly on Lighthouse accessibility audits.

When I'm not coding, I contribute to open-source accessibility tooling and speak at local web development meetups.
